Brentford Analysis

Participating in the prestigious Football Analytics Hackathon posed an exciting challenge: to thoroughly analyze a Premier League team and suggest two top transfer targets within a €60 million budget. Selecting Brentford as my focus, armed with the powerful Cube Platform and thorough research, I began my investigative journey.

It's worth noting that this challenge drew significant interest, with 425 applicants competing for participation. After a rigorous selection process, only 150 were chosen to take part. In this competitive environment, I employed insights gleaned from detailed analytics and personal observation to analyze Brentford's potential transfer targets.

Notably, my resulting report not only offers strategic insights for football analytics but also demonstrates resourcefulness; I opted not to exhaust the entire €60 million budget, recognizing Brentford's maximum highest transfer fee paid for a player stands at €33 million.
